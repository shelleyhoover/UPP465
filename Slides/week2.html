Refresher:

When we are showing differences in a feature's attributes we usually are making difference in type and/or quantity:

Points:

  • Points represent features as a single coordinate pair (x,y)
  • Points do not have area or length
  • Visualization Options: Fill Color & Pattern, Size, Symbol, Shape, Opacity, Outline Color, Pattern, and Thickness

Lines

  • Lines represent features as a series of 2+ coordinate pairs
  • Lines have length, but no area
  • Visual Options: Line Style, Width, Color, Opacity

Polygons

  • Polygons represent features as 3+ coordinate pairs that form an enclosed shape
  • Polygons have area
  • Visual Options: Fill Color & Pattern, Symbol, Opacity, Outline Color, Pattern, and Thickness
